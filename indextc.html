Overview

Have you ever quickly bought something and then regretted it right away? It's a common situation when shopping online. In the fast online shopping world, it's important to be able to fix your purchases. Amazon, the largest online store, gets this and simplifies Amazon cancel orders. If you're unsure how to cancel order on Amazon, this guide is your go-to!.

How To Cancel Order on Amazon: Step By Step

Following are the steps of the process of how to cancel order on Amazon:

Step 1: Log in to Your Amazon Account

Go to Amazon's homepage and click "Sign in." Enter your login details like email/phone number and password.

Step 2: Go to Your Orders

Once you're logged in, locate the "Returns & Orders" tab situated at the upper-right corner of the page. This section serves as your hub for managing your recent amazon cancel orders, including initiating cancellations.

Step 3: Locate the Order You Want to Cancel

Scroll through your list of amazon cancel orders to identify the specific one you wish to cancel. Remember, timing is crucial here - ensure the amazon cancel orders haven't entered the shipping process yet. If it has, you won't be able to cancel it directly.

Step 4: Click on Cancel Items

Once you've located the target amazon cancel orders, click on the "Cancel Items" button. This action marks the official beginning of the cancellation procedure.

Step 5: Choose a Reason for Cancellation

Amazon values customer feedback, even when canceling orders. Choose a reason for your cancellation from the dropdown menu provided. This input helps Amazon understand customer preferences and enhances its overall service quality.

Step 6: Confirm Cancellation

Finally, click "Cancel Selected Items" to confirm. Once confirmed, you've successfully initiated the cancellation process. You've now learned how to cancel an Amazon order before it ships!

How To Cancel Order on Amazon after Shipping

If your amazon cancel order has already shipped, follow these steps:

Step 1: Go to Your Orders

Like before, log in and head to "Returns & Orders."

Step 2: Choose Return or Replace Items

When you find the amazon cancel order, click on "Return or Replace Items." This is how you cancel an Amazon order that's already on its way.

Step 3: Select a Reason for Return

Pick a reason, just like you would for canceling.

Step 4: Choose How You Want to Return

Amazon gives you options for returning. Pick the one that suits you best.

Step 5: Print the Return Label

Print the label and stick it on the package. This label has what Amazon needs to handle your return.

Step 6: Send the Package

Drop the package at a place authorized by Amazon. Keep the receipt till you get your refund.

This way, even if the order has shipped, you can still cancel it by returning the package.
